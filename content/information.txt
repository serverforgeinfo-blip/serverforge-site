ABOUT SERVERFORGE

ServerForge is where digital communities are born.

We are a specialized two-person studio dedicated to crafting fully launch-ready Minecraft servers and Discord communities for creators, gaming groups, content creators, school clubs, businesses, and hobbyists who want to skip months of setup and start building their community immediately.

OUR STORY

ServerForge was founded by two passionate developers and community architects who were frustrated by how much time setup really takes. Building a high-quality Minecraft and Discord ecosystem can require hundreds of hours:

- Plugin configuration
- Gameplay balancing
- Role and permission systems
- Bot integrations
- World and spawn design
- Welcome and onboarding flows
- Moderation structure

We created ServerForge to remove that burden from clients so they can focus on growth, events, and community engagement.

WHAT WE FORGE

Every project is hand-crafted. We do not sell copy-paste templates. We build tailored ecosystems around your exact vision.

Examples include:

- Survival SMP with custom mechanics
- Minigame hubs
- Roleplay worlds
- Fully moderated Discord communities
- Standalone custom plugins
- Standalone data packs and resource packs

IMPORTANT SCOPE NOTE

We specialize in server-side work.

- Custom Discord bots are delivered as source code only (you host it).
- We can guide you through free or low-cost hosting options.
- We do not create custom Minecraft mods (Forge/Fabric).
- We do build plugins, data packs, resource packs, maps, and configurations.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

- Quality over quantity: every project gets full attention.
- Launch-ready, not launch-eventually: you can open the same day we deliver.
- You own everything: all files, configs, and assets are yours forever.
- Communication first: you stay involved throughout production.
