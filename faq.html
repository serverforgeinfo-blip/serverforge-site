FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything clients ask before ordering: what is included, timelines, ownership, payment, support, and custom requests.

What do I get with a complete package?

A fully configured Minecraft server plus a matching Discord server, themed together and ready to launch. This includes plugins, world setup, permissions, anti-cheat, roles, channels, bots, welcome flow, moderation tooling, and verification.

Do you provide hosting?

No. We focus on forging and setup. We can recommend trusted hosts and provide full deployment instructions.

Do you create custom Minecraft mods?

No. We do not build Forge/Fabric mods. We specialize in Paper/Spigot/Purpur plugins, data packs, resource packs, builds, and server configurations.

Do you build custom Discord bots from scratch?

Yes, as code delivery only. You receive a clean, documented codebase and host it yourself. We provide setup guidance and point you to options like Railway, Replit, or VPS hosting.

Can I request something completely custom?

Absolutely. Custom work is our specialty. Share your vision and we can forge custom plugins, data packs, bot code, gameplay mechanics, and full platform themes.

How long does an order take?

Scope-dependent: a small data pack may take 3 to 5 days, while full Minecraft + Discord packages usually take 1 to 3 weeks. We provide a precise timeline before starting.

What versions and software do you support?

Latest stable Paper, Spigot, and Purpur. If you are unsure, we will recommend the best option for your project.

Do you offer support after delivery?

Yes. Every package includes a free post-launch support window (typically 7 to 14 days by package size). Extended support is available as an add-on.

Can I buy only one service, like a plugin or Discord setup?

Yes. Everything is modular. You can order single products such as a plugin, build, data pack, Discord setup, or bot code.

Do I own the files and custom code?

100%. After payment is complete, all files, configs, plugins, builds, bot code, and Discord assets are yours with no hidden subscriptions.

Which payment methods do you accept?

PayPal only for now. Orders use a 50% deposit to secure your slot and 50% on final delivery.

What if I am not happy with the result?

We work with check-in points and revision rounds during production. A project is not marked done until you are satisfied with the agreed scope.

Can I resell what you build for me?

Personal or own-community use is unlimited. Reselling as-is is not allowed. Commercial licensing is available for agencies and resellers.

How do I get started?

Reach out with your idea. We respond with a quote, timeline, and a few clarifying questions before work begins.
