About ServerForge

ServerForge is where digital communities are born. We are a specialized two-person studio crafting fully launch-ready Minecraft servers and Discord communities for creators, gaming groups, streamers, schools, businesses, and hobbyists.

Our Story

ServerForge was founded by two developers and community architects who were tired of seeing people lose months to setup work. Configuring plugins, balancing gameplay, designing Discord structures, integrating bots, writing onboarding messages, and handling moderation systems can eat hundreds of hours.

We built ServerForge to remove that technical burden so clients can focus on community growth, events, and content instead of infrastructure.

What We Forge

Every server is hand-crafted, never mass-produced. We do not sell copy-paste templates. We build a tailored ecosystem around your exact goals.

  • Survival SMP setups with custom mechanics
  • Minigame hubs and roleplay worlds
  • Fully moderated Discord communities with onboarding flow
  • Standalone custom plugins, data packs, and branded systems

Our Philosophy

  • Quality over quantity: each project receives full focus.
  • Launch-ready, not launch-eventually: deliverables are ready to open same day.
  • You own everything: files, configs, code, and assets are permanently yours.
  • Communication first: you stay involved at each stage of the forge.

Scope Notes

  • We specialize in server-side work.
  • Custom Discord bots are delivered as code only; you host them.
  • We do not create custom Minecraft mods (Forge/Fabric).
  • We do not create texture packs.
  • We build plugins, data packs, maps, and server configs.

Who We Serve

Streamers, YouTubers, gaming communities, school clubs, indie studios, businesses launching gamified communities, and anyone who wants professional infrastructure without technical headache.
